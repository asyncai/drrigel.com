A Life Dedicated to Saving Lives

Dr. Darrell S. Rigel is a world leader in dermatology, recognized globally for his groundbreaking contributions to melanoma detection and skin cancer research.

He is the co-creator of the revolutionary ABCDE method for early melanoma detection, which has become the international standard for identifying potentially dangerous skin lesions and has saved countless lives worldwide.

With over 40 years of experience, Dr. Rigel has dedicated his career to advancing the field of dermatology through research, education, and patient care. He has delivered over 1,000 presentations at medical conferences worldwide and has authored more than 305 publications in professional journals.
